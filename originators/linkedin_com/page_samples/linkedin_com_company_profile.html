Endava is a well-established IT Services company, with over 700 full time employees working across its European development centres, London headquarters and further offices in the US. Formed in 2000, Endava is uniquely placed within the IT services sector as it employs an optimum blend of onshore consultancy, project management and nearshore IT service delivery.

Endava focuses on the banking, financial services, telecommunications, media, sports, entertainment and professional services sectors and can design, implement and manage secure, high-volume, business-critical systems and digital services. Endava services a wide range of big-brand customer across these sectors.

Specialties

Application Development, Application Managament, Testing, Digital Media, Hosting, Agile, Planned Iterative, Nearshore, Infrastructure Management
